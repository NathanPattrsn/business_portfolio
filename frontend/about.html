About Me

Hi, I’m Nathan — a passionate web designer and developer who creates engaging, user-friendly digital experiences.

I hold a BSc (Hons) in Web Design and Development from Edge Hill University and earned top marks in my Level 2 and 3 BTEC in Computer Science. I’m skilled in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and design tools like Figma and Photoshop.

Previously, I worked full-time as a web developer, where I built and maintained websites and systems for a range of clients, focusing on accessibility and responsiveness.

I enjoy blending creativity with problem-solving, from crafting clean Figma interfaces to coding layouts that work seamlessly across devices. I'm always looking to grow, collaborate, and build meaningful projects.

About Nathan Patterson

Hi, I’m Nathan, a creative and enthusiastic web designer and developer with a passion for building purposeful digital experiences that are both visually engaging and user-friendly.

My background includes a Level 2 and Level 3 BTEC in Computer Science, where I achieved a Triple Distinction Star, followed by a BSc (Hons) in Web Design and Development from Edge Hill University, graduating with a 2:2. Throughout my education, I developed a strong foundation in front-end technologies including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, alongside design tools like Figma and Adobe Photoshop.

I’ve had professional experience working in a full-time web development role, where I maintained websites and built business systems for a variety of clients. This role has strengthened my technical skills and deepened my understanding of creating accessible, responsive, and impactful web solutions.

I enjoy combining creativity with problem-solving, whether that’s designing clean, modern interfaces in Figma or coding layouts that adapt seamlessly across devices. I’m always looking for new opportunities to grow, collaborate, and work on projects that make a positive difference.
